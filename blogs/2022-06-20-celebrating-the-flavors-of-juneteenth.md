Adobe’s purpose is to enable creativity for all, and the Juneteenth commemoration opens up a wide range of opportunities for individuals to authentically depict their lives and experiences. We’re partnering with Adobe Stock artist O Koren, a nonbinary “photo ethnographer” whose work most often focuses on Black contributions to American foodways and popular culture.
